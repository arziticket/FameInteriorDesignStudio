About Us - Fame Studio

Designing Spaces with Heart, Craft, and Clarity

Based in the bustling city of Mumbai, Fame Studio is more than just an interior design firm—it’s a creative house where ideas meet craftsmanship and vision becomes reality. We believe that interiors should do more than look beautiful; they should inspire, comfort, and truly reflect the people who live in them. Every home or commercial space we design is carefully curated to combine elegance with functionality, ensuring it feels timeless today and adaptable for tomorrow.

Our work spans across luxury residential interiors, boutique commercial projects, and bespoke furniture design. What sets us apart is the way we listen—understanding your lifestyle, your aspirations, and even the smallest details that matter most to you. With this foundation, we create designs that are deeply personal, seamlessly practical, and rooted in global inspiration while celebrating Mumbai’s cultural richness.

Our work covers luxury homes, commercial projects, and custom furniture. What makes us special is the way we listen first—to your stories, your lifestyle, and your dreams. Every design we create is not just a project, but a reflection of the people who will live and work in it. The outcome is always personal, practical, and timeless..
